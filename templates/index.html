Placements Analytics and Dashboard



The Placements Analytics and Dashboard

This is a comprehensive web application designed to store and analyze placement data for students who have secured positions in software companies. The system offers powerful analytical features that enable users to enhance statistical reports and visualizations. A pie chart representation showcases the distribution of placements based on the technologies learned by students, and a comparative analysis of placement trends year by year is presented through bar graphs. Additionally, a pie chart representation of hiring companies illustrates the number of students recruited by each organization, providing valuable insights and industry preferences.



Technologies and Opportunities

Nowadays, there are a lot more technologies and opportunities in the software field. In order to get placed in a well-established software company, we need to find out which companies are well-established and which technologies need to be learned that are important. This visualization serves as a guide for students to identify the most sought-after skills in the industry, empowering them to make informed decisions regarding their educational and career paths. Furthermore, the project includes a comparative analysis of placement trends year by year, presented through bar graphs, and a pie chart representation of hiring companies illustrates the number of students recruited by each organization, providing valuable insights and industry preferences.
